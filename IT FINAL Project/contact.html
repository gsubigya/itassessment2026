How to Get Here

We are in Sanepa, a short drive or walk from central Lalitpur.

Coming From Route Approx. Time Landmarks
Kathmandu City Centre Cross Bagmati Bridge, turn left at Sanepa Chowk 15 – 25 min by car Look for the green NatureRoot sign on the right side of Nursery Lane
Patan Durbar Square Head north on Mangal Bazar road, past Lagankhel bus stop 10 – 15 min by foot
Tribhuvan Airport Taxi directly 25 to 35 min; fare approx. NPR 400 – 600 Ask the driver for "Lalitpur Nursery Lane"
Public Micro-bus Ratnapark to Lagankhel route; alight at Sanepa stop 30 – 45 min 5-minute walk from bus stop
