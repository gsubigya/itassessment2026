Welcome

Your Local Plant & Garden Centre

NatureRoot Nursery has been helping gardeners in Kathmandu and the surrounding valley find the right plants since 2009. Whether you are starting your first container garden or redesigning an established outdoor space, our team is here to guide you.

We have more than 400 varieties of trees, shrubs, flowering plants, vegetables, and herbs grown either on site or sourced from trusted local growers. Every plant is checked and labelled before it reaches our floor. Stop by any day of the week, or browse our services below to plan your visit.

What We Offer

Three Good Reasons to Visit

We keep things simple: great plants, honest advice, and fair prices.

Wide Plant Selection

From native Himalayan species to tropical houseplants, we carry varieties suited to Nepal's diverse climate zones. Each is clearly tagged with care instructions and sun requirements.

Expert Gardening Advice

Our staff includes trained horticulturists who can walk you through soil selection, seasonal planting schedules, pest management, and watering needs specific to your location.

Design Service

Need a full garden plan? Our design team visits your site, measures the space, and delivers a simple planting layout practical ideas that work.

Quick Reference

Seasonal Planting Guide

Not sure what to plant or when? Use this as a starting point. Timing may vary by altitude and climate.

Plant Category Spring (Mar – May) Autumn (Sep – Nov)
Sow / Plant Notes Sow / Plant Notes
Vegetables Tomatoes, Beans After last frost; needs full sun Spinach, Radish Cool-season crops; sow direct
Cucumber, Squash Warm soil required; water well Lettuce, Peas Light frost tolerant varieties
Flowering Plants Marigold, Zinnia Direct sow after frost; deadhead regularly Pansy, Dianthus Transplant seedlings; shelter from frost
Herbs Basil, Coriander Start indoors 4 weeks early Mint, Fenugreek Divide roots or direct sow
✳ Based on Kathmandu Valley conditions. Reconsider timing for higher elevations.
