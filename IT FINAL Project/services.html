Our Services

Plants, advice, and design everything your garden project needs in one place.

What We Do

Services We Provide

Whether you need a single plant or a complete garden, we have a service to match your goal and budget.

Plant Sales & Advice

Browse more than 400 varieties of trees, perennials, vegetables, herbs, and houseplants. Staff are always on hand to advise on plant selection, care, and placement.

Every plant is labelled with its common name, botanical name, sun and water needs, and mature size so you can make an informed choice without pressure.

Landscape Design

Our team visits your site, assesses soil and light conditions, and produces a planting plan. We focus on layouts that are easy to maintain once established.

Consultations are charged at a flat rate. If you purchase your plants through NatureRoot, the consultation fee is deducted from your bill. Contact us to arrange a site visit.

Seed Starting & Propagation

We run guided sessions every spring where participants leave with their own collection of seedlings. We also sell starter kits with everything you need to begin at home.

If you have a specific plant you would like propagated, bring us a cutting and our team will do their best to root it for you free of charge for regular customers.

Soil, Compost & Supplies

We stock a range of compost, fertilisers, pots, and tools. All our compost is made on site from plant waste and sold in reusable, nature- friendly bags. Bulk orders can be delivered within the valley.

We also carry organic pest-control products and beneficial insects for home gardens and greenhouses.

Free Monthly Workshops

On the first Saturday of each month we hold a free, hands-on workshop on a different gardening topic composting, growing in containers, seed saving, and making your own organic fertiliser.

Workshops last about 90 minutes and suit all levels. No registration needed just show up. Check our contact page or email us to be added to the reminder list.

Delivery & Planting

Large orders can be delivered to addresses within the Kathmandu Valley. We pack plants carefully so they arrive in good condition.

For an additional fee, our team can assist with planting. This is popular for tree deliveries and customers not yet comfortable with planting technique.
