About NatureRoot

A small nursery with a straightforward goal: more people growing more plants.

Our Story

Started with a Small Plot in Lalitpur

NatureRoot Nursery began in 2009 when Sunita Shrestha, a retired school teacher with a lifelong passion for plants, converted a corner of her family land into a small growing space. What started as a few dozen seedlings sold at the local market eventually grew into one of the Kathmandu Valley's most trusted plant nurseries.

Over the years, Sunita's children joined the business, each bringing new skills landscape design, agronomy, and business management. Today, NatureRoot occupies over 2,000 square metres of growing space and serves thousands of customers each year, from first-time balcony gardeners to professional landscapers.

We have always believed that gardening should be accessible to everyone. That belief shapes how we price our plants, how we train our staff, and how we talk to our customers. No complicated Latin names unless you want them just plain guidance on what will grow well in your space.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Every decision we make from which plants to stock, to how we train new staff comes back to a few core beliefs.

  • ✔ Quality over quantity. We would rather stock fewer varieties and know them well than overwhelm customers with options they cannot care for.
  • ✔ Honest advice. If a plant is wrong for your space, we will tell you even if it means a smaller sale.
  • ✔ Local sourcing. We grow most of our stock on site and buy the rest from farms within Nepal wherever possible.
  • ✔ Sustainable practices. We use compost-based growing media, avoid synthetic pesticides in our greenhouse, and recycle nursery pots.
  • ✔ Community. We run free monthly workshops on topics like composting, seed saving, and kitchen-garden planning.
